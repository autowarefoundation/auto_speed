# Contributor On-boarding Guide

Thank you for your interest in helping contribute to our work at Autoware Foundation to develop an open-source End-to-End AI system. To get started, please follow the steps below:

## How to Get Started:
- **Give this Github repo a 'star'** and review the README file to familiarize yourself with our work

- Join our Discord, and in the 'robotaxi' work group channel, please add a brief message to introduce yourself. 
  - Here is the link to join our Discord: https://discord.gg/Q94UsPvReQ
  - And this is the link to the [robotaxi work group discord channel](https://discord.com/channels/953808765935816715/1501613700799397919)

- Catch up on our latest discussions and meetings. Here is a [link to our working document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1jxtpbGiqwdyOVmjCfHKe726hXnhcgFT9c6NiYQXAPbQ/edit?usp=sharing) and the [link to our whiteboard](https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVHXGPW7o=/?share_link_id=799206057355)

- **Join and participate in the Robotaxi work group meetings** to learn about our developments, get started in understanding the codebase, hear from maintainers and industry experts, and begin making code contributions. We meet every week on Wednesday. There are two meeting slots which you can join, (Slot 1 is more suited for people joining from East Asia) (Slot 2 is more suited for people joining from Europe/USA) - *we cover the same agenda items in both slots so you only need to join one of them as best fits your schedule.*
